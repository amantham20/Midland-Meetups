Walter vs. Wizards

A wave-based brawler. Fight through the castle wall and fair grounds, bank crystals from defeated wizards in the tower chest, and spend them at the altar to unlock spells.

Arrow keys to move, Up to jump or climb the tower ladder, Space to sword-strike or cast your active spell, number keys 1–5 to switch spells once unlocked.
